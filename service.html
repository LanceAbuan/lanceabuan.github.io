How Lance Gives Back


Clothing Donations

Lance gives back to his community by collecting well-loved clothes from individuals and families that do not need them anymore. Lance has worked with multiple churches and groups to gather clothes so that they can prepare, package, and ship no longer needed clothes to the Phillippines, where it's distributed to those who do not have any. Clothing Drives are a recurring event that Lance and his family have done together for years to pass the good fortune that they've received from the opportunity to be naturalized U.S. Citizens. Through the collective efforts of his family and those who generously give, they have successfully donated hundreds of pounds of clothes to those who need clothing.

Beautification Efforts

Because of his adventurous personality, Lance loves observing Nature, and especially loves observing Nature when she is at her healthiest. To ensure that Nature can stay in her best form, Lance loves participating in nature beautification projects, where people voluntarily come together and form groups to clean up areas. This involves going around and collecting litter and cleaning areas with hoses. His favorite places to clean are where the best photos can be taken, such as parks and lakes. He plans on taking before and after pictures for future projects to inspire others by showing how much of a difference they can make in their areas.

Food Bank

In addition to delivering clothes to those in need, Lance often volunteers at Food Banks, especially at the Houston Food Bank whenever he is in the area. His first time volunteering was with his Church, and he appreciated how Food Banks are such a powerful way to support those in need. Depending on what food bank he goes to, he's responsible for different jobs, such as packaging food to be shipped to other Food Banks, or preparing bags to hand out food. Because of his experience with cooking and previously working as the head chef of a food truck, Lance wishes to do more than prepare food and one day volunteer to cook hot food for the hungry at help centers. If there are any opportunities, please contact him about it.
