Lance Abuan - Full Stack Developer

Lance Abuan is a First Generation immigrant from Lucban, Quezon in the Philippines.

He moved at a young age and grew up in Laredo, Texas, and spent the majority of his childhood there. Eventually, he moved to The Woodlands, Texas, and attended Oak Ridge High School. In High School, he originally planned on being a Statistics major until he took an Introduction to Programming class where he was exposed to Java. He fell in love with the logic of Computer Science and decided it was what he wanted to pursue.
